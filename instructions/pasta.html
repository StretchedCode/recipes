pasta

A meal for the sophisticated.

Pasta has been a staple in Italian cuisine since the beginning of their era. This simple meal consisting of carbohydrates and pasta sauce allows for the most exquisite fusion of flavours to come together with the creators choice of meat and vegetables.

All one needs to have some pasta at home is some boiling water!

Ingredients

  • 350 oz Cabelone Pasta
  • 250g Tomato Sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil one litre of water for a few minutes
  2. Place the pasta within the water. Wait 7 minutes
  3. Pour tomato sauce on top of the pasta. Enjoy!
